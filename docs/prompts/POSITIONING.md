# CERNIQ Product Positioning Prompt

> Ensures marketing and messaging stay disciplined.

## What CERNIQ Is (Externally)

CERNIQ is **bilingual ALM reporting software** for cooperativas and credit unions.

## Core Value Proposition

**Upload balance sheet → generate bilingual ALM report.**

One sentence. No qualifiers.

## Messaging To Avoid

Do **not** use any of the following in external communications:

- ❌ "AI platform"
- ❌ "Quant trading"
- ❌ "Financial OS"
- ❌ "Analytics platform"
- ❌ "Bloomberg-style"
- ❌ "Enterprise intelligence"
- ❌ "Institutional operating system"

Those are long-term capabilities. They are not today's product.

## What To Say

- ✅ "Fast, credible ALM reporting"
- ✅ "Upload your balance sheet, get a board-ready report"
- ✅ "Bilingual ALM reports for cooperativas"
- ✅ "Replace manual ALM spreadsheets"
- ✅ "Professional risk reports in minutes, not weeks"

## Internal vs External

| Layer | Message |
|---|---|
| Internal | Enterprise ALM analytics project with a path toward broader institutional risk infrastructure |
| Design partners | Experimental ALM analytics engine for cooperativas, built with early institutional partners |
| Public | Helps institutions generate bilingual ALM reports through secure balance-sheet upload |
