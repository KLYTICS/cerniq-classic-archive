# CERNIQ Core Product Mission

> Internal alignment prompt — every agent, engineer, and designer must internalize this.

## Mission

CERNIQ is building the most intuitive institutional financial intelligence platform.

The wedge product is **bilingual ALM reporting for cooperativas and credit unions**.

## User Journey

The user journey must remain extremely simple:

1. Upload balance sheet
2. CERNIQ processes and validates data
3. ALM engine runs
4. Bilingual report generated
5. Download board-ready report

## Operating Principles

- Every engineering, design, and infrastructure decision must support this workflow first.
- The system must feel credible to CFOs, risk officers, and regulators.
- Avoid complexity in the user surface.
- Deep complexity belongs in the backend engine.
- CERNIQ must eventually replace internal quant spreadsheets and consultant-driven ALM cycles.

## Long-Term Vision

CERNIQ becomes the **operating system for institutional balance-sheet intelligence**:

- ALM
- Liquidity Risk
- Treasury Analytics
- Regulatory Intelligence
- Portfolio Risk

But the **public wedge stays**:

> Upload balance sheet → receive bilingual ALM report.

No feature creep until traction.
