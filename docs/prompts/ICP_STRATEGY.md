# CERNIQ ICP Strategy Prompt

> For sales outreach and go-to-market execution.

## First Buyers

| Segment | Priority |
|---|---|
| Puerto Rico cooperativas | 🔴 Primary |
| US credit unions | 🟡 Secondary |
| CPA firms serving financial institutions | 🟢 Channel |

## Buyer Personas

| Persona | Pain |
|---|---|
| **CFO** | Needs board-ready reports without manual assembly |
| **Risk Manager** | Needs defensible ALM metrics for regulators |
| **Treasury Manager** | Needs rate sensitivity analysis faster than current cycle |
| **Financial Consultant** | Needs a repeatable tool to serve multiple institutions |

## Their Pain

- Manual ALM spreadsheets that take weeks to build
- Dependency on expensive external consultants
- Slow reporting cycles that miss committee deadlines
- Monolingual reports that don't serve bilingual leadership

## CERNIQ's Answer

CERNIQ replaces manual reporting with a repeatable **upload-to-report workflow**:

1. Upload balance sheet CSV
2. System validates and normalizes data
3. ALM engine computes risk metrics
4. Bilingual PDF generated automatically
5. Download and present to board

## Outreach Approach

- Lead with the free sample report offer
- Use COSSEC public data to personalize outreach
- Target the CFO or Director Financiero by name
- Send bilingual messages (Spanish preferred for PR cooperativas)
- Follow up at day 3 and day 7
