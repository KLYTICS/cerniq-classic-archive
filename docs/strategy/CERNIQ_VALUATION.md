# CERNIQ Valuation Memo

Date: 2026-04-05

## Executive View

Based on the current repo state, pricing, and go-to-market wedge, CERNIQ is best valued today as an **early-stage vertical fintech / regtech SaaS** with a live product, a narrow ICP, and early revenue-design-partner positioning.

## Recommended Working Valuation

**Base working valuation today: $1.8M pre-money**

Use this as the default internal number for strategy, partner conversations, and prioritization until there is stronger revenue proof.

## Suggested Range

- Conservative floor: **$1.2M**
- Base case: **$1.8M**
- Upside this week if pilot proof lands cleanly: **$2.5M-$3.0M**

## Why This Range Fits

### Product Readiness

- CERNIQ has a live frontend, live backend, billing, portal flows, and bilingual reporting.
- The product is narrow enough to sell as a real wedge, not just a concept.
- The repo already contains a documented operating path to paid pilots.

### Market Thesis

- The strongest current wedge is Puerto Rico cooperativas, with credit unions and CPA/advisory firms as adjacent expansion channels.
- The product solves a recurring, compliance-adjacent workflow with high manual cost and slow turnaround.
- Bilingual delivery and Puerto Rico-specific framing create real strategic differentiation.

### Revenue Posture

Current repo pricing implies a simple commercial ladder:

- Pilot: `$750`
- Platform: `$299/mo`
- Partner: `$499/mo`

That makes CERNIQ easy to test, but it also means valuation should stay disciplined until recurring conversions are visible.

## Scenario Math

### Scenario A: Proof-of-Product Only

- 3 paid pilots = `$2,250` one-time revenue
- 0 recurring conversions
- Implied valuation: **$1.2M-$1.5M**

### Scenario B: Early Commercial Validation

- 3 paid pilots
- 5 platform customers at `$299/mo`
- 1 partner customer at `$499/mo`
- Monthly recurring revenue: `$1,994`
- Annualized recurring revenue: `$23,928`
- Implied valuation: **$1.8M-$2.5M**

### Scenario C: Strong Near-Term Wedge Proof

- 5-8 platform customers
- 2 partner customers
- steady pilot inflow
- Annualized recurring revenue roughly `$30K-$45K+`
- Implied valuation: **$2.5M-$3.5M**

## What Raises The Valuation Fastest

- Converting the first 3-5 institutions from pilot to recurring.
- Landing 1-2 CPA/advisory partners.
- Demonstrating a clean upload-to-report-to-payment workflow on live production.
- Collecting one or two usable testimonials from real institutions.

## What Keeps The Valuation Capped

- Reliance on a narrow geography without visible expansion proof.
- Product breadth that exceeds the public story and creates focus drift.
- Missing proof assets around the pilot workflow.
- Operational drift between what production does and what the repo claims.

## Decision

Until new revenue evidence arrives, run CERNIQ as a **$1.8M pre-money company with a $1.2M-$3.0M working range**.

That number is high enough to reflect a functioning product and a real niche, but conservative enough to stay honest before recurring revenue compounds.
