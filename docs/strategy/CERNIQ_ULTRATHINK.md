## 1. Purpose

This document is the **single source of truth** for the **Ultrathink** theme: a memorable wrapper for *analytical depth, board-grade rigor, and bilingual ALM readiness*—without repositioning CERNIQ as an “AI company” or violating public wedge discipline.

**Ultrathink is not a separate product category.** Public truth remains: *bilingual ALM reporting software; upload balance sheet → board-ready report.*

---

## 2. What Ultrathink Is (and Is Not)

| Ultrathink **is** | Ultrathink **is not** |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| A **campaign narrative** for depth, completeness, COSSEC-aligned output | An “AI platform” or “intelligence OS” pitch |
| Language for **multi-scenario, regulator-ready thinking** (stress, sensitivity, audit trail) | A claim about generic LLM or “extended thinking” product features unless explicitly shipped to users |
| An optional **future name** for a premium bundle/tier tied to real differentiation | A SKU committed in code without product confirmation |
| A **metaphor** for the care a strong internal analyst or consultant applies—compressed into minutes | Replacement for the one-line wedge: *upload → bilingual ALM report* |

## 4. Three-Layer Framework

### Layer A — Campaign / narrative (primary use now)

**Job:** Differentiate on **rigor and completeness** of the ALM output (modules, stress paths, bilingual board PDF), not on “being AI.”

**Core story:** Leadership still needs the same *quality of thinking* that expensive engagements promise; CERNIQ delivers that **workflow**—secure upload, validated metrics, reproducible runs—at pilot-to-recurring economics.

### Layer B — Optional named tier or bundle (future)

Use the name **Ultrathink** for a paid bundle **only** if product clearly delivers extra value, for example:

- Expanded scenario / stress coverage vs. base tier
- Shorter SLA or priority support
- Multi-entity or firm-level features for CPA partners

Until then, keep “Ultrathink” as **marketing language** only.

### Layer C — “Deep reasoning” positioning (metaphor)

Map “depth” to **institutional behaviors buyers already trust**:

- Multiple risk lenses (e.g., duration gap, NII sensitivity, liquidity framing as appropriate to your modules)
- Audit-friendly, repeatable runs (not one-off consultant PDFs)
- Spanish and English for bilingual boards and regulators

Avoid comparing to consumer “reasoning modes” or AI model brands.

## 6. Voice & Language (EN / ES)

- **PR cooperativas:** Spanish-first for social, DMs, and email when the recipient is local; English acceptable for bilingual executives or mainland adjacency.
- **Credit unions (US):** English-first; mention bilingual where it differentiates.
- **Tone:** Confident, institutional, never hype. Numbers and outcomes over adjectives.
- **“Ultrathink” usage:** Sparingly—often lead with outcome (“informe listo para junta”) and use Ultrathink in headline or kicker, not every sentence.
