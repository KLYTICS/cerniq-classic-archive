## What is CERNIQ?

CERNIQ is a bilingual (ES/EN) **Asset-Liability Management (ALM)** intelligence platform purpose-built for Puerto Rico's cooperativas, credit unions, and community banks. It automates the entire ALM cycle — from raw balance-sheet CSV upload to a board-ready, COSSEC-compliant PDF report — collapsing a process that previously cost **$15,000+ per consultant engagement** into minutes of automated computation.

**Stack in one line:** Swift 6 + SwiftUI (macOS/iOS) · Next.js 16 + React 19 (web) · NestJS 11 + Prisma 7 + PostgreSQL 15 (backend) · Railway + Vercel (infra) · OpenAI GPT-4o (AI)
