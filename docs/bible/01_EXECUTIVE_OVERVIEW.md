## 1.1 What is CERNIQ?

CERNIQ is a bilingual (Spanish / English) **Asset-Liability Management (ALM)** intelligence platform purpose-built for Puerto Rico's cooperativas, credit unions, and community banks.

It automates the ALM analysis process that institutions currently perform manually or pay consultants **$15,000–$30,000 per engagement** to produce. The platform collapses a 3–6 week analyst process into minutes of automated computation and delivers a board-ready, COSSEC-compliant PDF report.

**Core capability:** Upload a balance sheet CSV → get a **14+ page board-ready ALM report** with Duration Gap, NII Sensitivity, EVE, LCR, Monte Carlo stress testing, and COSSEC compliance assessment — in minutes, not weeks.

## 1.3 Market Context

**The addressable universe:**
- ~100+ COSSEC-regulated cooperativas in Puerto Rico
- Collectively manage **tens of billions in assets**
- Deeply underserved by financial technology — no native ALM software built for this market
- Regulatory pressure from COSSEC increasing: institutions face annual ALM reviews, exam preparation cycles, and board reporting requirements

**Why cooperativas specifically:**
- Member-owned structure → CFOs and board members are accountable to members, not shareholders → demand for clear, audit-ready reporting is extremely high
- Puerto Rico cooperativas operate in Spanish first — existing mainland ALM software is English-only and ignores local regulatory requirements (COSSEC ≠ NCUA)
- Average cooperativa: $100M–$500M in assets, 3–10 person finance team — cannot afford full-time ALM analysts

## 1.4 Why Apple Platforms Now

The macOS/iOS native app is a **Phase 1 retention and activation lever**, not an ancillary feature:

1. **User profile match** — Puerto Rico cooperativa CFOs and board members skew heavily toward Apple devices (iPhone + MacBook)
2. **Friction elimination** — Native navigation removes the browser-startup barrier for the 5–10 most critical ALM workflows (submit data, check report status, view compliance score)
3. **Offline-capable preview** — macOS app enables secure preview of ALM outputs without opening a browser; critical for board meetings with poor connectivity
4. **Enterprise credibility signal** — App Store presence on macOS/iOS signals enterprise-grade seriousness comparable to Bloomberg Terminal or Refinitiv Eikon
5. **First-mover advantage** — No other ALM platform in the PR cooperativa space ships native Apple apps
6. **Biometric auth** — Face ID / Touch ID on iOS dramatically reduces login friction for field analysts running reports between client visits
7. **Push notifications** — "Your ALM report is ready" push at the moment the PDF is generated → drives instant re-engagement vs email which may be ignored for hours

5. **Outbound intelligence** — 6-agent autonomous sales pipeline continuously identifies and reaches Puerto Rico cooperativas that have not yet been contacted
6. **Network effects** — As more cooperativas onboard, CERNIQ's benchmark data (anonymized) makes risk rating comparisons more accurate — value increases with scale
