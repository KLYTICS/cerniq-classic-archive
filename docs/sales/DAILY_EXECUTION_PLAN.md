## WHEN THINGS AREN'T WORKING (Diagnostic)

| Signal | Cause | Fix |
|--------|-------|-----|
| <20% LinkedIn acceptance | Profile not optimized or pitching in request | Rewrite headline, remove pitch from connection msg |
| <5% email open rate | Subject line weak or sender reputation low | Test 3 subject lines, continue warmup emails |
| <2% email reply rate | Body too long, no personalization, weak CTA | Shorten to 4 lines, add their asset figure, ask yes/no question |
| 0 demos after 50 emails | Wrong contact (not CFO), wrong timing, or value prop unclear | Switch to calling openers, try different persona (Risk Officer vs CFO) |
| Demos don't convert | Demo too long, didn't discover pain first, asking for too much | Shorten to 15 min, ask "how do you do ALM today?" first, offer $750 pilot not subscription |
| High no-show rate on demos | Booked too far out or insufficient confirmation | Book within 48h, send WhatsApp reminder morning-of |
