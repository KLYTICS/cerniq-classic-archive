# CERNIQ 30-Day Operator Playbook

## Objective

Turn CERNIQ into a narrow, credible, sellable wedge:

**Bilingual ALM reporting software for cooperativas and credit unions.**

This document is the operating plan for the next 30 days. It is intentionally narrower than the broader codebase. The goal is revenue proof, not feature breadth.

## Positioning

### Core sentence

CERNIQ helps cooperativas and credit unions generate professional ALM risk reports in minutes through secure balance-sheet upload, bilingual reporting, and compliance-ready output.

### What CERNIQ is not saying publicly right now

- Not a broad institutional intelligence platform
- Not a quant analytics operating system
- Not a multi-product finance suite
- Not a SpendCheck-first company

### Public headline direction

**ALM reports for cooperativas and credit unions**

Upload your balance sheet. Generate a bilingual, compliance-ready risk report in minutes.

## Public Product Scope

For the next 30 days, the public product should emphasize only these surfaces:

- Landing page
- Pricing page
- Demo request
- Login
- Institution creation
- CSV upload
- Report generation
- Report download
- Portal and status views

Everything else stays backstage.

## 14-Day Execution Plan

### Days 1-3: Production Hardening

Goal:

**A real prospect can go from landing page to report without embarrassment.**

Checklist:

- Verify database setup and migrations
- Run signup, auth, institution creation, upload, processing, download flow
- Verify Stripe checkout
- Verify email delivery
- Verify portal states and transitions
- Verify report generation does not stall
- Improve unclear error messages
- Add basic monitoring for failed jobs and stalled reports

Success criteria:

- Two clean end-to-end demos in a row
- No manual database patching during demo
- No broken state between upload and report delivery

### Days 4-5: Public Positioning Rewrite

Goal:

**The homepage explains the wedge in five seconds.**

Homepage sections:

- Hero
- Who it’s for
- What report they get
- How it works
- Why bilingual and compliance-ready matters
- Pricing
- Demo CTA

Required copy themes:

- Cooperativas
- Credit unions
- Secure upload
- Bilingual reporting
- Board-ready PDF
- Pilot path

### Days 6-7: Proof Assets

Goal:

**Sell with proof, not just software.**

Assets to produce:

- One flagship sample report
- One 60-90 second walkthrough video
- One one-page explainer PDF

Proof checklist:

- Sample report is clean enough to send cold
- Video shows upload to report workflow only
- One-pager explains problem, workflow, output, pricing, and bilingual advantage

## Sales Motion

### ICP priority

1. Puerto Rico cooperativas
2. CPA and advisory firms serving cooperativas or credit unions
3. Community-bank treasury or finance teams

### Founder pitch

CERNIQ helps cooperativas and credit unions generate bilingual ALM risk reports through a secure upload workflow instead of waiting on expensive manual consulting cycles. We’re starting with a focused reporting product for Puerto Rico and early pilot institutions.

### Outreach assets needed

- Two short outreach email variants
- One Spanish-language version
- One partner / CPA version
- Sample report link
- Walkthrough link

## Pricing

Lead with only these paths:

- Pilot / single report: $750
- Recurring platform access: $299/mo

Partner pricing can remain available, but it should not be the primary offer until proof accumulates.

## Metrics That Matter

Track weekly:

- Institutions contacted
- Reply rate
- Calls booked
- Pilots started
- Pilots completed
- Paid conversions
- Days from first contact to pilot
- Upload-to-PDF completion rate
- Upload-to-PDF turnaround time
- Onboarding friction notes

- One sample report drafted

### Week 2

- Walkthrough video complete
- One-pager complete
- 30-50 target institutions listed

### Week 3

- Founder-led outbound live

### Week 4

- 3 pilots active or complete
- 1-3 paid conversions targeted
- 2 usable testimonials or quotes collected

## Operating Rules

- Hide feature breadth publicly
- Keep the wedge sentence consistent across homepage, emails, calls, and demos
- Do not lead with architecture depth
- Do not expand the product story before pilot feedback
- Treat sample report quality as a sales asset, not a side artifact
