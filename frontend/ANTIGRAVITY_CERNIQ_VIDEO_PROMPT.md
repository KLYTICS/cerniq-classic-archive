# CERNIQ Antigravity Video Prompt

Create a polished product demo video for **CERNIQ**, a white-background, electric-cyan branded institutional finance platform.

## Goal
Show that CERNIQ is a real working solution for:
- real-time market data
- portfolio risk analytics
- valuation and screening
- options analytics
- execution quality
- ALM and institutional reporting

The video should make the product feel:
- credible
- institutional
- fast
- readable
- modern

Avoid generic AI visuals, abstract sci-fi scenes, or dark cyberpunk tropes. Keep the branding aligned with the live product:
- white background
- clean glassy cards
- blue/cyan accents
- black or near-black text
- CERNIQ logo and orbital symbol preserved

## Format
- Length: 45 to 60 seconds
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Style: premium SaaS/product demo with cinematic polish but grounded UI realism
- Motion: smooth camera drifts, subtle zooms, light interface transitions, clean wipes

## Narrative Structure
1. Open on the CERNIQ homepage.
Show the logo, the white environment, and the core headline.
Overlay message:
"One platform for market intelligence, portfolio risk, valuation, and execution."

2. Transition into market search.
Show a user searching a ticker, opening a security view, and watching live market data update.
Overlay message:
"Search live markets and move directly into analysis."

3. Show portfolio risk analytics.
Reveal VaR, volatility, stress testing, and correlation views.
Make it obvious the interface is built for institutional workflows, not retail investing.
Overlay message:
"Measure risk with clarity."

4. Show valuation and options workflows.
Move into valuation/screener views, then options analytics and Greeks.
Overlay message:
"Validate the thesis before capital is deployed."

5. Show execution quality and reporting.
Display execution dashboards, analytics summaries, and a clean report/export state.
Overlay message:
"Turn analysis into decision-ready output."

6. Close on the CERNIQ brand and a final product summary.
Overlay message:
"CERNIQ. Intelligence Engineered."

## Visual Direction
- Use UI-first storytelling. The product must be the hero.
- Prefer readable layouts over flashy motion.
- Text should always be crisp, dark, and legible.
- Keep spacing generous.
- Make data visuals feel real: charts, tables, metrics, watchlists, modules.
- No clutter, no tiny unreadable labels, no exaggerated neon effects.

## Important Product Signals To Show
- real market tickers and search
- dashboard modules
- risk charts and portfolio analytics
- valuation or screening views
- options analytics
- execution reporting
- enterprise-grade reporting or summary output

## Voice and Tone
Confident, institutional, modern.
This should feel like a platform a serious investment team, treasury group, or risk team would actually buy.

## Ending Frame
Show the CERNIQ logo on a white background with subtle blue/cyan motion accents.
Final text:
"Market data. Risk analytics. Valuation. Execution. One CERNIQ platform."
