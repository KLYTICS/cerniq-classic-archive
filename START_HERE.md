### ✅ Project 1: Risk Parity Portfolio - AI Infrastructure
**Location:** `projects/01-risk-parity-ai-infra/`

**What it does:**
- Optimizes portfolio weights for equal risk contribution (not equal dollars)
- Covers AI supply chain: Semiconductors, Cloud, Networking, Power
- Interactive Streamlit app with backtesting and visualization

**Technical highlights:**
- `risk_parity.py`: Convex optimization engine using CVXPY
- `app.py`: Full-featured Streamlit dashboard
- Compares risk parity vs equal weight vs market cap weight
- Risk contribution decomposition and correlation analysis

**Demonstrates:**

### ✅ Project 2: VaR/CVaR Risk Report Generator
**Location:** `projects/02-var-risk-reports/`

**What it does:**
- Generates institutional-grade risk reports for any portfolio
- Calculates VaR (95%, 99%) and CVaR using historical and parametric methods
- Shows max drawdown, rolling volatility, worst days, correlation matrix

**Technical highlights:**
- `risk_engine.py`: Statistical risk calculation engine
- `app.py`: Interactive report generator with export capabilities
- Supports multiple confidence levels and risk-free rate adjustment
- Q-Q plot for normality testing

**Demonstrates:**
- Understanding of institutional risk management
- Statistical rigor (VaR, CVaR, stress testing)
- Production engineering (error handling, modular design)
- Data visualization skills
